Delete Account

Deleting account will delete all your data including Name, Email, Delivery Address and your Order history. Once deleted this data cannot be retrieved.
If you are sure to delete your account follow the below steps.

  • Go to Profile section in Bytes App
  • Tap on Settings
  • Select 'Delete Account'
  • Select 'Yes' in confirmation dialog
  • Complete OTP verification
  • Account Deleted !
